A Dogs Purpose: A Novel for Humans (Paperback Book)

$15.99

Availability: 3 in stock

Category: Books about Dogs
  • Description

This book is written by W. Bruce Cameron. This product listing is for the paperback version of the book.

A Dog’s Purpose: A Novel for Humans is a powerful story that follows the life of a single dog who was reincarnated four times, living through hardship, love, loss, and adventure. Although the dog was named Toby in his first life as a feral puppy, he was renamed when he was in the hands of a new owner. In all the dog’s lives, he encounters people who greatly impact each experience he goes through. Cameron combines humor, heartbreak, and triumph as Toby navigates all four of his lives, figuring out what it means to be a dog to different people, all of whom are walking on different paths. This novel is the first in the “A Dog’s Purpose” series.
